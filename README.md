# NAIMAN

**The after-dinner pearl.**

NAIMAN is a premium cardamom & saffron breath freshener — tiny silver-coated botanical seeds carried in a pocket tin. Not a mint, not a gum, but a finishing course. The modern digestif for people who notice the details.

## The Brand

The name comes from the Arabic benediction *Na'eeman* (نعيماً), spoken after someone refreshes themselves — a haircut, a shower, a renewal. It means "may you enjoy this freshness." Every pearl carries that tradition forward.

**Target audience:** Design-conscious, food-curious 22–38 year olds — the kind of person who notices packaging.

**Positioning:** If Aesop and Supreme had a baby that sold breath fresheners. Streetwear editorial meets luxury apothecary.

## Visual Style

- **Palette:** Coral/burnt orange (`#E8573A`), matte black (`#111111`), warm cream (`#F5F0EB`), metallic silver accents
- **Typography:** Cormorant Garamond (display/editorial serif) paired with Space Grotesk (clean modern sans-serif)
- **Aesthetic:** Confident, unhurried, room to breathe. No rounded-corner card grids, no SaaS gradients, no startup energy. Every spacing choice and font weight is intentional.
- **Photography:** Editorial lifestyle and macro product shots — Midjourney-generated, curated for the layout
- **Copy tone:** Sensory, evocative, lowercase-leaning. Short sentences. No exclamation marks. No buzzwords. Every word earns its place.

## The Site

A single-page marketing splash hosted on GitHub Pages. Single HTML file, inline CSS/JS, no build step.

**Sections:** Hero with email capture, The Ritual (concept), The Product (ingredients), The Origin (Na'eeman story), Waitlist CTA, Footer.

**Forms:** Both email signup forms submit to [Formspree](https://formspree.io) for waitlist collection, with a redirect-based thank-you confirmation state.
